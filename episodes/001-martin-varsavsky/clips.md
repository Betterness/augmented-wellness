## 1. The bank that rejected him — then funded him `~4:11`

> "The same bank, Midland Bank rejected me for a job and gave me a 9 million
> dollar loan that was my first breakthrough in life."

*Why it clips:* a perfect, self-contained contradiction — and a window into the
"defiant and difficult" younger Martin who told interviewers he saw himself, in
five years, "at least as your boss." **Guardrail:** this $9M is a 1980s
real-estate *loan*, not Certuma money (see [`claims.md`](./claims.md), Claim 3).

## 2. "My wife can't tell if it's me or Pedro" `~45:02`

> "It's me to the point that my wife Nina who's very active on [X] too can many
> times not tell if I posted or Pedro posted."

*Why it clips:* the single most arresting line on AI voice fidelity — and the
through-line to the artifact. Pair it with: "now I have two LLMs there has to be
an agreement of two different LLMs… in order to post something." **Guardrail:**
Pedro is *disclosed* ("this is not a secret I've disclosed it"); the clip is
about voice fidelity, not a license to drop disclosure (Claim 4).

## 3. Cycling at 66 `~22:32`

> "It's also a remarkable that at 66, I don't have any pains or aches or injuries…
> I had maybe 120 stitches… but not fractures."

Pair with: "I cycle maybe 14 hours a week." *Why it clips:* the "how he lives a
full life" half of the show in one number. **Guardrail:** one person's anecdote,
not a training plan; not medical advice (Claim 5).

## 4. AI as co-founder — a seed round with no employees `~34:00`

> "I ended up raising the 10 million seed at a 50 million pre without any
> employees. It was just the AI and I."

Pair with the kicker: he asked the AI which VC would understand a "medical AI
that passes the FDA," it said 8VC in Austin, "so I flew to Austin to see them…
and I said we take the whole round." *Why it clips:* the cleanest possible
statement of agentic founding. **Guardrail:** true *at the raise* — he then
"hired a great team" (Claim 8).

## 5. "I am a doctor, but I'm not a doctor" — the Certuma origin `~34:00`

> "GPT says, but I'm not a doctor… [Claude], I'm not a doctor… [Grok], I'm not a
> doctor… Gemini… I'm not a doctor… They behave like doctors. And then when you
> want the prescription, they say, I'm not a doctor."

*Why it clips:* the frustration that launched the company, in his own cadence.
**Guardrail:** Certuma's *goal* is FDA approval, it is **not approved**, and it
keeps a physician in the loop (Claims 1–2).

## 6. Racing your 2010 self — "U versus U" `~25:53`

> "I've gone up the same mountain… maybe 200 times… I can see my climbing better
> than my average, way better than my average, worse than my average. That's the
> U versus U… where I'm ranking against myself since 2010."

*Why it clips:* a quietly profound reframing of self-quantification — not beating
others, beating your past self. **Guardrail:** Stravar is a private, unreleased
app (Claim 6).
