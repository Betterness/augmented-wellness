# Medical Claims Policy

better-x may discuss AI, health, wellness, wearables, labs, GLP-1s, longevity, nutrition, and research, but it must be careful.

## Rules

- Do not diagnose.
- Do not claim cures.
- Do not imply AI replaces a clinician.
- Do not give personalized medical advice to strangers.
- Use source links for research claims.
- Distinguish product perspective, personal experience, and scientific evidence.

## Good Framing

- "This is interesting because..."
- "One practical implication might be..."
- "For builders, the opportunity is..."
- "This does not replace medical advice, but it points to..."
