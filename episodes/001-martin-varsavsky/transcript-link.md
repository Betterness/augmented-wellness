# Transcript — Episode 001: Martin Varsavsky

The canonical, editorially-reviewed episode page lives on the polished site:

**→ https://betterness.ai/augmentedwellness/001-martin-varsavsky**

That page is the source of truth for the watchable/listenable episode, the
finished transcript, and chapter links.

## Why the full transcript is not pasted here

This companion repo is the **hands-on lab**, not the publication. The working
transcript we used to ground these notes is an **auto-generated draft pending
editorial review** — it contains the usual ASR artifacts (e.g. "Sertuma"/"Sir
Tuma" for **Certuma**, "Mungaro" for **Mounjaro**, "Stravar"/"Stagaba Cloud"
mishearings, mangled investor names). Pasting it verbatim would:

1. spread mis-spellings and mis-attributions as if they were fact, and
2. duplicate content that belongs on the canonical episode page, which is where
   corrections land.

So instead of the raw transcript, this package gives you the **grounded
extracts**:

- [`show-notes.md`](./show-notes.md) — summary, who Martin is, the arc, chapters
- [`sources.md`](./sources.md) — every company/tool/person, lanes intact, with
  `[verify]` flags on anything not yet confirmed
- [`claims.md`](./claims.md) — load-bearing claims, each with its guardrail
- [`clips.md`](./clips.md) — the most clippable moments with timestamps
- [`artifact.md`](./artifact.md) — what `better-x` is and why it came from Pedro
- [`companion-agent-page.md`](./companion-agent-page.md) — the agent-readable
  version of all of the above

## For editors

The working ASR draft is held with the episode production assets, not in this
public repo. When the canonical page's transcript is finalized, treat **that**
as authoritative and reconcile any `[verify]` items in `sources.md` against it.
Quotes in this package are either verbatim from the working draft or clearly
framed as paraphrase; re-check verbatim quotes against the finalized transcript
before quoting them anywhere load-bearing.
