# Episode 002 — Ulisses Abbud: Ride + Recovery Intelligence

This is the public artifact for Augmented Wellness episode 002 with Ulisses Abbud.

The episode's central idea is simple and useful: recovery is not the reward after performance. Recovery is the adaptation mechanism that allows performance to improve.

## What is included

- `artifacts/the-art-of-longevity-ulisses-abbud.pdf` — Ulisses's original presentation deck.
- `research-analysis.md` — Betterness analysis connecting the episode themes to the local Ironman / endurance research corpus.
- `questions/recovery-prompts.md` — prompts to use with Bett-i, a coach, or your own agent.
- `starter-prompts.md` — practical prompts for turning training, HRV, sleep, and recovery signals into daily actions.

## How to use this

Start with the deck, then read the research analysis. The goal is not to diagnose yourself from wearable data. The goal is to ask better questions: What is my body adapting to? What is the cost of today's stress? What should change today so tomorrow's training or work actually lands?

## Guardrails

This artifact is educational. It is not medical advice, training prescription, or diagnosis. Use it with a qualified coach or clinician when decisions affect your health, training load, medications, injury recovery, or medical conditions.

## Source posture

The research analysis is based on a local Betterness working corpus created for Augmented Wellness from PubMed discovery buckets around Ironman, triathlon, ultra-endurance, fueling, hydration, cardiac risk, recovery, injury, wearables, and training load. It cites PubMed records by PMID and link. It does not include copyrighted full-text papers.
