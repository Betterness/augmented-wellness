# Recovery Prompts

Use these with Bett-i, a coach, or a personal agent. Replace bracketed fields with your own context.

## Daily readiness

```text
You are helping me think like a high-performance coach, not a doctor.
Here is my context: sleep [x], HRV [x], resting HR [x], training yesterday [x], work stress [x], soreness [x].
What is the most likely recovery signal here? What should I change today?
Give me: 1 green-light option, 1 conservative option, and 1 red-flag question I should consider.
```

## Nervous-system reset

```text
My HRV is lower than usual and I feel wired.
Suggest a 5-10 minute breathwork or downshift protocol that is safe, simple, and not medicalized.
Explain why it may help in plain language.
```

## Training load conversation

```text
I am training for [event/goal].
My last 7 days: [sessions].
My recovery signals: [sleep/HRV/resting HR/mood/soreness].
Where am I most likely under-recovering? What question should I ask my coach?
```

## Nutrition and recovery

```text
Analyze my last 24 hours of fueling for endurance recovery.
Training: [session]. Food: [food log]. Sleep: [sleep].
Where might I be underfueling, overcorrecting, or missing protein/carbohydrate timing?
Keep it practical and non-diagnostic.
```
