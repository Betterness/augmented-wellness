# Artifact — Ride + Recovery Intelligence

Episode 002 leaves a practical public artifact around Ulisses Abbud's core idea:
recovery is not the reward after performance. It is the adaptation mechanism.

## Included

- `artifacts/the-art-of-longevity-ulisses-abbud.pdf` — Ulisses's deck on stress, recovery, resilience, and longevity.
- `research-analysis.md` — Betterness synthesis connecting the episode to the local Ironman / endurance research corpus.
- `questions/recovery-prompts.md` — prompts for turning HRV, sleep, training load, stress, and subjective readiness into better questions.
- `prompts.md` — builder prompts for a recovery summary, ride-file analysis, coach agent, and research assistant.

## What to build

A recovery-intelligence assistant that can read training context, wearable signals,
and subjective stress, then return bounded next actions:

- what looks strained;
- what may be under-recovered;
- what to ask a coach or clinician;
- what one small action could improve today's recovery.

## Guardrails

This is educational. It is not a medical device, diagnosis, training prescription,
or replacement for a coach or clinician. Wearable data is useful, but noisy; the
artifact is designed to improve questions, not to pretend certainty.
